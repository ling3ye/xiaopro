## Design Notes

This fan shroud is specifically designed for the ESP32-S3 development board, accommodating a standard 40mm × 40mm cooling fan.

### Features

- **Snap-fit Design**: No screws needed — clips directly onto the dev board
- **Optimized Airflow**: Simulated duct design effectively covers the main SoC area
- **Great Compatibility**: Does not block GPIO headers or interfere with wiring
- **Print-friendly**: No supports needed, works well at 0.2mm layer height

### Print Recommendations

- PLA or PETG recommended
- 20% infill is sufficient; increase to 40% for extra strength
- 0.2mm layer height works well; 0.16mm for smoother surfaces
