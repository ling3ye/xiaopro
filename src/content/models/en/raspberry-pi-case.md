## Design Notes

A custom protective case for the Raspberry Pi 5, balancing aesthetics and functionality.

### Features

- **Precise Cutouts**: All ports perfectly aligned — USB, HDMI, Ethernet, and more
- **Cooling Vents**: Top and bottom ventilation holes, supports 30mm fan installation
- **GPIO Access**: Open side panel for GPIO connections, expansion boards, and jumper wires
- **Snap Assembly**: No screws needed — top and bottom shells clip together

### Print Recommendations

- PLA recommended for best results
- 15% infill is sufficient
- Supports needed for bottom clip area only
- Light sanding after printing improves fit
